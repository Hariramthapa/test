What makes DES different?

Covid-19 has changed the world and has forced all of us to make changes in how we live and work. Here at DES Employment Group, while we have remained open and continue to service companies like yours, we’ve made changes as well.

So what sets us apart?

  • At DES we evaluate talent, potential, and fit of candidates through our extensive virtual interviewing process.
  • We follow ALL CDC guidelines with all potential employees. Temperatures are taken on everyone and all must wear masks to enter our offices.
  • DES guarantees employees and will never send out an individual who isn’t qualified for the job.
  • All applicants are E-verified and checked on sex offender registries.
  • We verify our applicant’s past employment and perform criminal background checks. DES also offers drug screening and other testing if requested.
  • Our recruiters will follow up with our employees and our clients on a regular basis to ensure everyone’s ultimate satisfaction.
  • We require all employees to complete an orientation process consisting of people skills and health and safety concerns.
  • DES provides on-call customer support 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. Our staff is considered on-call for you at all time, no automated voicemail, but a personal phone number that ensures your priorities are our priorities.
